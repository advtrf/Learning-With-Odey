Greetings!

Hello everyone, congratulation you are the chosen one who has traveled all the way here. Anyway talking about science, for me, science education has never been simply about memorizing formulas or repeating established theories. Science feels like a deeply human way of uncovering the mysteries of the universe. It gives us the tools to ask meaningful questions about the world and, through careful observation and evidence, gradually move closer to the truth. In that sense, learning science is not just an academic responsibility but a personal journey toward understanding.

When we truly engage with science, we gain more than correct answers. We develop a sense of wonder. Realizing that the movement of planets, the growth of plants, and even the behavior of light follow patterns that can be understood changes the way we see our surroundings. It reminds us that the world is not random or chaotic, but structured in ways we are capable of exploring. This awareness naturally leads to appreciation. To me, science education should cultivate that awareness, helping learners become not only knowledgeable, but also more attentive, reflective, and respectful toward the world they inhabit.

About This Website

Learning with Odey was built as an interactive science learning resource for Grade 8 junior high school students (SMP), following the Kurikulum Merdeka. It covers Additive and Addictive Substances through interactive materials, inquiry-based experiments, a quiz, educational mini games, and more - all aligned with SDG 3.

The website uses a student's sketchbook aesthetic: warm, handwritten fonts, notebook lines, and a calm color palette - because learning should feel safe and approachable, not stressful. Odey serves as a friendly guide throughout, adding humor and encouragement along the way.
